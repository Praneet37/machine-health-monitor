About This Demo

This is a demonstration predictive maintenance platform. All machine sensor readings (temperature, vibration, pressure, RPM) are simulated for showcase purposes — no real industrial hardware is connected. The AI prediction engine uses rule-based health scoring to estimate machine status, failure probability, and remaining useful life.
